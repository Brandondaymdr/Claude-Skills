# Thumbnail Templates by Series

Each series should have a consistent thumbnail layout so viewers instantly recognize the content type.
Use these as your starting PSD templates — one per series.

---

## Vinyasa Flow

**Layout:** Face + Title (Pattern 1)
- Photo: Carla mid-flow or smiling, cropped chest-up
- Background: Warm white or soft sage gradient
- Title color: Warm Charcoal `#2E2B28`
- Text: "[Duration] Vinyasa Flow" in Gill Sans Light
- Accent: Thin horizontal rule above title in Warm Taupe

---

## Yin Yoga & Restorative

**Layout:** Action Shot + Overlay (Pattern 2)
- Photo: Full body in a grounded yin pose (forward fold, pigeon, etc.)
- Overlay: Soft sand at 35% opacity
- Title color: White or Warm White
- Text: "Yin Yoga · [Duration]" in Gill Sans Light
- Mood: Slower, softer — use more negative space

---

## Meditation & Breathwork

**Layout:** Minimal / Text-Forward
- Photo: Carla seated with eyes closed, OR abstract nature (water, sky)
- Background: Deep Sage or Warm Charcoal overlay at 50%
- Title color: Warm White `#F9F5F0`
- Text: "Guided Meditation · [Duration]" in Gill Sans Light, high tracking
- No loud graphic elements — serene and spacious

---

## Morning Flow

**Layout:** Split Layout (Pattern 3)
- Left: Carla photo, bright and energetic expression
- Right: Warm White background block
- Title: "[Duration] Morning Flow" in Gill Sans Light, Warm Charcoal
- Sub-label: "All Levels" in smaller Gill Sans Light with increased tracking
- Accent: Small sun icon or horizontal accent line optional

---

## Beginner Yoga

**Layout:** Face + Title (Pattern 1)
- Photo: Welcoming, approachable expression — Carla smiling
- Background: Soft pastel (blush or light sage)
- Title: "Beginner Yoga · [Duration]" in Gill Sans Light
- Badge/label: "BEGINNER FRIENDLY" in small Gill Sans Light caps, tracked wide

---

## Yoga Challenges (e.g., 7-Day, 30-Day)

**Layout:** Bold / Series Branding
- Photo: Dynamic pose or confident expression
- Background: Richer color — Deep Sage or Warm Taupe
- Title: "[X]-Day Yoga Challenge" in Gill Sans Light (larger scale, ~110px)
- Day number badge: Circle shape with "Day 1", "Day 2", etc. — update per video
- Consistent badge position: Top-left corner

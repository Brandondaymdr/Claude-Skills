# Carla Gentile Yoga — Brand Style Reference

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Use Case |
|------|------|--------|----------|
| Primary | Gill Sans Light | Light | All thumbnail titles, subtitles |
| Emphasis | Gill Sans Regular | Regular | Class type labels, duration text |
| Strong Accent | Gill Sans Bold | Bold | Use sparingly for 1–2 key words only |

**Font Size Guidelines (at 1280×720px canvas):**
- Main title: 80–110px
- Subtitle / class type: 50–65px
- Small label / duration: 36–48px

**Tracking (letter-spacing):**
- Gill Sans Light looks best with slightly increased tracking: +20 to +50
- ALL CAPS titles: use +75 to +100 tracking for an airy, premium feel

## Photography Style

- **Lighting:** Soft, natural light — avoid harsh flash or gym lighting
- **Background:** Minimal — studio walls, nature, clean interiors
- **Wardrobe:** Neutral, earth tones, solid colors (avoid busy patterns that compete with text)
- **Expression:** Warm smile OR serene/eyes-closed for meditation content
- **Angle:** Eye level or slight elevation — avoid low angles
- **Cropping for thumbnails:** Frame from chest up for face shots; full body for pose shots

---

## Logo Usage

- Place logo in a consistent corner (bottom-right recommended)
- Minimum size: 80px tall on a 720px canvas
- Use white or light version on dark/photo backgrounds
- Use dark version on light/white backgrounds
- Always maintain clear space around logo equal to its height

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Use Gill Sans Light for all text
- Keep text to 5 words or fewer
- Use consistent layout templates per series
- Check thumbnail at small size (mobile preview)

### ❌ Don't
- Use script, decorative, or serif fonts
- Crowd the image with too much text
- Use harsh neon or oversaturated colors
- Rasterize text layers (keep editable)
- Flatten the PSD before saving the master
